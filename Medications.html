Transport of medicines and medical products

The transportation of medicines and medical equipment has been the basis of our business since 2010.

We are one of the leading companies in this field. Our extensive experience and continuous development is the reason we are here as well as our experienced logistic team to solve and fix problems as quickly as possible. We have formed a team of experienced and qualified drivers with regular training courses to raise professional efficiency.

Medicines and pharmaceutical products are very sensitive and therefore we ensure continuous, real-time online monitoring of our thermal compounds through temperature transmission messages and an alarm in case of deviations for immediate intervention from us.

We aim to transport medicines, medical preparations and medical tools from warehouses to pharmacists and laboratories with high professionalism and accuracy, with strict confidentiality, etc., in the best possible conditions.

Pharmaceutical products in particular are particularly sensitive. Here standard temperatures from 2°C to 8°C and from 15°C to 25°C are strictly observed.

Transportation in severely frozen areas is also possible.All pharmaceutical products or even sensitive genetically modified drugs most of which (such as vaccines) must be transported in an uninterrupted cold chain Not a problem for us, we protect the effectiveness of your medicines.

Non-Tamper documents: Certified temperature monitoring in our vehicles ensures tamper-proof documentation of every transport. Temperature records are archived by us and can be printed if required. You will receive a copy of this with your transportation invoice as proof of no cooling interruption. Flexible working movement Direct shipments, parcels or boxes are delivered by us as direct flights as quickly as possible with temperature control on demand. Benefit from our years of experience 24 hour service, 365 days a year for continuous availability. We look forward to meeting your wishes and requirements. Which we are pleased to achieve through our wide range of offers.
